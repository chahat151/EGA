Euro Glass & Aluminium

SYDNEY BASED, Euro Glass & Aluminium Pty Ltd provides premium custom made structural grade aluminium – vertical bar (Types C or D) or fully framed glass balustrade – balustrading (Types A or B) systems crafted with proactive engineering using specific & highly accurate measuring equipment,superior design and tasteful aesthetics.

*Our structural grade aluminium has apart from the above mentioned high strength, good weldability and really excellent corrosion resistance to salt water to where the majority of our residential & strata refurbishment works are located so you know we got you covered straight from the word go….. our materials are heat treated and artificially aged to T6 temper strength. With plenty of experience under our belt we focus on our passion to deliver you the very best in everything we manufacture / install & it shows as an end result!

There is one particular thing we don’t disregard when it comes to qualifying our work for your best interests & that is “Time” As a matter of fact we spend so,so much of this product on all our work that it actually pays to end up using us for any of your aluminium & glass requirements otherwise your simply just missing out……No such thing as get it in & get it out-(No, No – Not here ! First class or nothing……& trust me you can see it)

It’s a win,win scenario for you, as we at Euro Glass & Aluminium know that good customer service does’nt end when you have made your purchase & all we ask as a favour after your jobs been installed is to post an honest Google Review for us, so that others can see what separates the men from the boys!
